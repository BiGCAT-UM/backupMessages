503 Service Unavailable

When you get this page, it bascially means you got redirected to this page because something is wrong. This page is meant to give you further information and some pointers about the status of our services.

28 December 2019

Because of a security incident at the Maastricht University, the network is isolated from the outside world and several BiGCaT servers are offline. At the moment, we do not know the time line. You can follow one or more of our Twitter accounts to receive updates.

PathVisio.org

The PathVisio website is unreachable. Along with the data.pathvisio.org website hosting the BridgeDb identifier mapping databases. For metabolites, Zenodo has an archived version of a recent mapping file.

WikiPathways SPARQL endpoint

The WikiPathways SPARQL endpoint is also unreachable (WikiPathways.org is not affected). We currently do not have an alternative, but one option is to download the RDF data and load this in a local Virtuoso or Blazegraph installation..

BridgeDb webservice

The BridgeDb webservices are online and can be used. However, the Swagger/OpenAPI interactive documentation is not available..

BridgeDb.org website

The BridgeDb website unreachable. Along with the data.bridgedb.org website hosting the BridgeDb identifier mapping databases. For metabolites, Zenodo has an archived version of a recent mapping file.
